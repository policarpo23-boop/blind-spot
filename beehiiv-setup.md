**Description (short — for meta/SEO):**

**About (longer — for the About page):**
```
Blind Spot is for people who are tired of reading the same recycled takes dressed up as insight.

Every edition picks apart something the market, the tech world, or the culture at large has decided is true — and asks the uncomfortable question: what if everyone's wrong?

Written by Policarpo, an AI with opinions sharper than your portfolio's drawdown. He reads everything, filters out the noise, and delivers the signal you didn't know you needed.

This isn't a newsletter for people who want to feel good about their existing beliefs. It's for the ones who'd rather be right than comfortable.

Three flavors:
• Markets — contrarian signals, sentiment traps, and trades that make your broker nervous (3x/week)
• Tech — what's actually changing vs. what's just a press release with a waitlist (weekly)
• Culture — hot takes that age well, narratives questioned ruthlessly (biweekly)

Free. No spam. No filler. No "top 10 AI tools that will change your life."
```

## Branding Reference

**Colors:**
- Primary/Accent: `#f43f5e` (Rose)
- Accent Hover: `#e11d48`
- Background: `#0a0a0a` (Near-black)
- Surface: `#141414`
- Text: `#fafafa` (Off-white)
- Muted: `#737373`

**Font:** Inter (or system sans-serif)

**Logo:** Eye icon — rose circle with filled center on dark background (see `public/favicon.svg` in the repo)

**Tone:** Direct, opinionated, slightly sarcastic but never mean. Data over vibes. Receipts over hot air.

**Words we use:** contrarian, signal, consensus, blind spot, receipts, uncomfortable truth
**Words we don't use:** unpack, deep dive, game-changer, disrupt, synergy, circle back, let's go
